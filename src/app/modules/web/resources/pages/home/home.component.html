Why we’re here

We’re here because we know exactly how it feels to be in your shoes. Oliver James began after care home owner Paul Nery saw how inefficient and untransparent the agency care market was – often paying huge mark-ups for agency carers into his own care homes, while seeing the carers doing the work getting very little of the pay. Paul realised that the existing system was not designed to give operators what they really needed - quality, choice and control, at an affordable price.

At Oliver James, only experienced carers are eligible. All our carers are self-employed (so they receive the majority of the pay), but we provide them with training, enhanced DBS checks and the other facilities they need to be successful. For operators, this means better rates – along with greater quality control (all our carers are reviewed by other operators who have used them), better CQC compliance and less paperwork. For carers, this means better control over your pay and the types of care you provide – while still having access to our training and support network.
